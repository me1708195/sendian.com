Welcome to Sendian Group

Today businesses operate in an unprecedentedly fluid environment. Every company operating in multiple countries needs a local trusted partner for executing projects. Qatar Master is one such time tested and trusted business partner of many Multi-National Companies operating in Qatar.

Established in 2007, Qatar Master is now a leading entity in Qatar for trading, contracting and workforce solutions. We have partnered with many Multi-National Companies to execute many high profile construction projects in Qatar.

Electromechanical Maintenance

MEP CONSTRUCTION

  • We provide general MEP construction design and installation services of electrical, HVAC (heating, ventilation, air-conditioning), firefighting systems, plumbing, drainage and water supply networks, delivering safe, comfortable and environmentally-sustainable building services to our clients.

  • We are committed to innovation and excellence. We continuously strive to make improvements as we progress – in size, domain, and credibility.

  • Another evident factor which makes EMS stand apart is our unique infrastructural advantage, availability to deliver the best resources, high quality machinery, support vehicles and equipment.

  • Our operations team, ranging from project manager to site engineers to supervisors and foremen, ensure that a project of highest quality is delivered in strictly controlled time frames. Proper coordination between the different departments internally provides the necessary platform for the smooth running of the entire operation.

    • MEP MAINTENANCE

  • Sendian offers specialized preventative maintenance programs with value added customizable maintenance service options. With proper and professional maintenance of equipment and facilities we provide systematic inspection, detection, and correction of incipient failures either before they occur or before they develop into major defects.

  • Preventative maintenance includes tests, measurements, adjustments, and parts replacement, performed specifically to prevent faults from occurring and to sustain adequate operating condition of equipment and facilities.

  • Our customizable maintenance programs are designed to preserve and restore equipment reliability. Preventive maintenance activities include partial or complete repairs which are performed and scheduled on regular basis.
